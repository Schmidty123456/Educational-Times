About

The Educational Times was a British journal published in the nineteenth century that addressed a wide variety of topics including a section in which readers can propose mathematical questions and submit answers to the questions that have been submitted. The mathematical questions and answers that appeared in the Educational Times were later extracted, reorganized (so that a question and its solutions are together), and published in a different journal entitled Mathematical questions with their solutions, from the "Educational Times"; we refer to this later journal as MQ. Dr. Jim Tattersall of Providence College, Rhode Island, USA then created a spreadsheet of the meta-data about these questions and answers. The meta-data in his spreadsheet includes for each mathematical question submitted to the Educational Times a single row that contains:

1) the question identifier (all the questions submitted have an assigned number in chronological order).
2) the name of the proposer
3) the date and volume of the issue of the Education Times in which the proposal of the question appears
4) the names of each solver of the question
5) the dates and volumes of the issues of the Educational Times in which solvers of the question submit their solutions
6) the corresponding volumes in MQ of the proposal of the question and of the solutions to the question
7) the question type (that is, the category within mathematics that the question resides)

The intent for this website is to provide an online tool to search the meta-data contained in the spreadsheet that Dr. Tattersall developed.
