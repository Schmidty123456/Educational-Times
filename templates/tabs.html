About

The Educational Times was a journal published in the 19th century that addressed a wide variety of topics, the main focus of this site being the mathematical questions and their answers. Later the questions and answers that appeared in the Educational Times were published in a different journal entitiled Mathematical questions with their solutions, from the "Educational Times", which is referred to as MQ. The intent for this page is to create an online tool to search data about these mathematical questions and their solutions, data that was collected by Dr. Jim Tattersall of Providence College. The data included here in the Search tab contains the question number, the volume in which the problem we published, the name of the person who proposed it, the names of the solvers (if any), the volume(s) the solution(s) were published in, the dates the volume(s) of the solutions were published, the volume of MQ that the problems and their solutions appear, and a number that refers to the question type.
Please refer to the Contacts tab for any questions/concerns.
(Note: The names may often be pseudonyms)
